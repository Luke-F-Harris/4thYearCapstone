What is PIRATEAI?

PIRATEAI is an artificial intelligence programming challenge, where two players build AIs that battle one another in the dangerous seas of Programus.

How to play.

The game consists of two players' AIs. Islands will be scattered throughout a randomly generated map. Each player will start with three Pirate Ships. Ships can dock to islands to gain production towards more ships. Each team's ships will be able to attack the other team's ships. Your AI will be tasked with guiding your ships to victory.
