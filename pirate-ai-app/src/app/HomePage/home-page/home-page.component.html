PIRATEAI encorporates the desire of programming, gaming, and competition. Battling head-to-head with your own designed artificial intelligence to prove victorious. PIRATEAI is a zero-sum game where one winning player will increase their Elo rating, and the losing player will decrease their Elo rating. An Elo rating is a method for calculating the skill level of players. Your AI will be matched against other AIs with a similar Elo rating.
